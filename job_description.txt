Power Automate Intern Job Description

We are looking for a Power Automate Intern with interest in workflow automation,
Microsoft Power Platform, and AI integration.

- Basic understanding of Microsoft Power Automate
- Knowledge of Power Apps and Dataverse (preferred)
- Experience with automation workflows
- Understanding of REST APIs and connectors
- Interest in AI models, Azure OpenAI, or LLMs
- Basic Python or scripting knowledge
- Good problem-solving skills

Responsibilities:
- Build and optimize Power Automate workflows
- Integrate automation with AI services
- Assist in building RPA and intelligent automation solutions
- Work with data sources like SharePoint, Dataverse, APIs
- Document workflows and automation logic
